CA2RXU - NRF52840
LoRa APRS Firmwares

💡 Instructions:

1. Select the board's firmware and click "Download Firmware".
2. Connect your board to PC. Double press board's RST button.
3. Copy the .uf2 firmware file into the board's folder on the PC.
4. (Board will restart automatically after this copying is complete).
5. Go to the chosen Web Configurator for your station.
